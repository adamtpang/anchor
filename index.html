Working Framework

A Conjecture on Value Creation

Building on Karl Popper and David Deutsch's epistemology of critical rationalism— problems are soluble through the creation of new knowledge. This framework applies that principle to business: identifying problems, conjecturing solutions, finding people, and constructing offers.
